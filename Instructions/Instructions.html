Kit Instruction Archive


As a service to modelers everywhere I have established this Instructions Archive. As time allows I will add various kit instructions here by Manufacture. I cannot count the number of times I have seen random modelers asking for instructions for kits they have acquired that lack them. Many of you may have helped out these guys in the past by scanning sets. Should you care to contribute those scans to this archive they must be of at least 100 DPI in JPEG format and can be emailed to me at Instructions@SwannysModels.com. As soon as I can review them they will be posted for the benefit of all.
