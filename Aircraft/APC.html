        Lubos Vinar, proprietor of Hobbyshop.cz is producing his own line of ALPS printed decals. Theses decals demonstrate good print registry and color density. He offers markings in 1/72 and 1/48 for several esoteric subjects. In addition he has some nice instrument decals ranging from 0.8mm to 2.2mm diameter useful for most scale instrument projects. If you need Hamilton Standard propeller logos in either 1/72 or 1/48, he can help you with those also. Prices of these decal sheets run anywhere from $10.00 US for some of the more complex sheets to as little as $1.00 US for instrument markings and propeller markings. For those of us here in the States shipping costs may not make these the best choice if all you are looking for is some decals but they make great additional items for when you are ordering the more unusual kits that can be found in Lubos� inventory.
