My local newspaper, the Sandusky Register, sent a reporter and photographer out to my house several weeks ago and interviewed me for about two and a half hours. Today, December 18, 2005 the article was printed. Due to numerous requests from other modelers and various friends for copies of the article I have scanned it in its entirety and posted it here for everyone’s enjoyment.
















        Well, there it is folks, my fifteen minutes of fame. Hope you enjoyed the article; I certainly had fun talking to these people and hope that this kind of stuff helps to grow our hobby. My wife's first comment was (laughing) "and your closet is in there too!"
